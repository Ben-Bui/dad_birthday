Detailed information about Daniel Dũng Bùi’s early life is not widely published. However, according to interviews with businesspeople:

He is known for bringing the American skincare brand Murad to Vietnam in the early 2010s.

Together with his wife, Daisy Dung, he built a nationwide distribution system for Murad. They trained spas and partners across the country.

Their business focused on:

Developing a spa system

Training skincare technicians

Distributing Murad products

After several years, their network grew to include thousands of partner spas and beauty agents in the beauty industry.

👉 In short:
He is not the founder of Murad, but the person who introduced the brand to Vietnam and built its distribution network there.
